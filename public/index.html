SMUN: Chapter I

Emerging as one of Kolkata’s most distinguished Model United Nations experiences, SMUN stands not merely as a conference, but as the genesis of a legacy. Chapter One marks the inception of a forum destined for greatness — where diplomacy is reimagined, excellence is uncompromising, and the pursuit of global discourse is elevated to an art.

Here, delegates do not just debate; they craft narratives of change, forge resolutions with conviction, and embody diplomacy at its most profound. With a spectrum of dynamic committees and a Secretariat devoted to vision over routine, SMUN Chapter One is the foundation of a tradition that aspires to transcend boundaries.

This is not simply the launch of an event — it is the dawn of an institution, a cause, and a movement whose story every delegate will help script.
